# Privacy Policy for PexelBulker

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

## Overview

PexelBulker ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and what we don't do with your information.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Access

The Extension requires certain permissions to function:

1. **Active Tab (`activeTab`)**
   - **Purpose**: To detect if you're on a Pexels.com page
   - **What we access**: Current page URL (only on pexels.com)
   - **When**: Only when you click the extension icon
   - **Data retention**: Not stored, only used in real-time

2. **Cookies (`cookies`)**
   - **Purpose**: To authenticate API requests to Pexels
   - **What we access**: Pexels.com cookies only
   - **When**: During video scanning and downloading
   - **Data retention**: Not stored by the extension, read directly from browser

3. **Downloads (`downloads`)**
   - **Purpose**: To save videos to your computer
   - **What we access**: Chrome's download API
   - **When**: During video download process
   - **Data retention**: Videos saved locally on your device only

4. **Storage (`storage`)**
   - **Purpose**: To save download progress and settings
   - **What we store**: 
     - Download queue
     - Current download index
     - Progress statistics
     - Download state (paused/active)
   - **Where**: Chrome's local storage on your device only
   - **Data retention**: Until you uninstall the extension or clear Chrome data

5. **Tabs (`tabs`)**
   - **Purpose**: To communicate with Pexels pages
   - **What we access**: Tab ID and URL of active Pexels tabs
   - **When**: During URL detection and API requests
   - **Data retention**: Not stored

### Host Permissions

The Extension requires access to these domains:
- `https://www.pexels.com/*` - Main Pexels website
- `https://videos.pexels.com/*` - Pexels video CDN
- `https://images.pexels.com/*` - Pexels thumbnail CDN

**Purpose**: To fetch video information and download video files

## Data Storage

All data stored by the Extension is:
- **Local only**: Stored on your device in Chrome's local storage
- **Temporary**: Used only for maintaining download state
- **Your control**: Deleted when you uninstall the extension
- **Not transmitted**: Never sent to any external server

## Data Security

- All operations happen locally on your device
- Communication is directly between your browser and Pexels.com
- We don't act as a middleman or proxy
- Extension uses secure HTTPS connections
- No passwords or sensitive information is stored

## Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with:

**Pexels.com**
- We use Pexels' public API to fetch video information
- We use Pexels' CDN to download video files
- Your interaction with Pexels is subject to [Pexels Privacy Policy](https://www.pexels.com/privacy-policy/)
- We don't collect or store any data from these interactions
